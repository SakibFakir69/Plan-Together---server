Nice Features for a Family Workspace
✅ Assign tasks to a specific family member
✅ Shared grocery lists
✅ Recurring chores (daily/weekly/monthly)
✅ Bill reminders
✅ Shared calendar
✅ Shopping checklist
✅ Task comments
✅ Due dates and notifications
✅ Progress tracking
✅ Activity history ("Dad completed Grocery Shopping")

For a family productivity app, the core workflow is usually:

Create a shared workspace.
Invite family members.
Create categorized tasks.
Assign tasks to members.
Set due dates or recurring schedules.
Notify assigned members.
Mark tasks complete and keep a history of activity
